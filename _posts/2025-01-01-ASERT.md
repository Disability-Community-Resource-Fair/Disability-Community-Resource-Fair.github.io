The ASERT Collaborative  (Autism Services, Education, Resources and Training) is a statewide partnership that provides streamlined access to information to the Pennsylvania autism community. ASERT offers resources covering a wide range of topics for self-advocates, caregivers, professionals, and community members while also providing information on events, support groups, and free online trainings.
