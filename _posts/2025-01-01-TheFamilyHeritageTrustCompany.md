The Family Heritage Trust Company provides professional trust administration and investment management services for individuals and families. We specialize in Special Needs Trusts and help families plan for the long-term financial security of loved ones with disabilities while protecting eligibility for important government benefits. Our team offers guidance on responsible asset management, fiduciary oversight, and planning options designed to support stability and quality of life.
