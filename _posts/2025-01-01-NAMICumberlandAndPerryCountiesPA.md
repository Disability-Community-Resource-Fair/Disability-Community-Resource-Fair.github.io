NAMI (National Alliance on Mental Illness) is the largest grassroots mental health organization in the nation. NAMI Cumberland and Perry Counties PA is the local affiliate of this organization. We are dedicated to improving the quality of life for people with mental illness and their families through support, education, and advocacy.
