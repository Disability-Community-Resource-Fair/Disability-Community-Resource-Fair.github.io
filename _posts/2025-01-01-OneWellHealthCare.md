OneWell Health Care provides person-centered in-home and community based supports and services to individuals with IDD (21+). Our In Home and Community Based Supports (IHCBS) include Companion, Respite, Behavioral Health, Therapy Services, Supported Employment, Home Modifications, Uber Transportation Services and more through ODP Waiver Funding.
