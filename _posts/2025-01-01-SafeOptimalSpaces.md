Safe Optimal Spaces uses occupational therapy skills to assess the safety of any space, most common is the home. With a unique skill set, we help guide our clients toward their goals, whether that is aging in place, fall prevention, ease or accessibility, or improved function. Our process includes an initial consultation, in-depth home safety assessment, and an home safety action plan.
