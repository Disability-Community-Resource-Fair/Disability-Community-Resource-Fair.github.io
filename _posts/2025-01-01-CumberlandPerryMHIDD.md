Cumberland-Perry MH.IDD is an alliance of three organizations sharing the mission of empowerment and community support; Mental Health (MH), Intellectual & Developmental Disabilities (IDD), and Early Intervention (EI). MH.IDD offers services to people of all ages with serious mental illness or intellectual and developmental disabilities.
