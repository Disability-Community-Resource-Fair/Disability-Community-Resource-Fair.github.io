Developmental Fitness has a passion for assisting those with developmental or intellectual disabilities reach their goals, dreams and aspirations. Developmental Fitness Company serves as their partner to a greater well-being through physical fitness. Throughout the course of our training, we also help them take a step higher in achieving their personal level of independence, learning how to make life choices, and becoming an important member of the community.
