3 & 4. The Bodhana Group

Services: Behavioral Services, ABA Therapy, Assessment & Treatment, Social, Recreational, Fitness, Camps, Therapeutic Services

Age Groups: Elementary (Grades K-6), Secondary (Grades 7-12), Post Secondary (High School and Beyond)

The Bodhana Group works to integrate modern tabletop gaming into therapeutic and capacity building goal work. We run groups, 1:1 counseling and offer assistance to organizations and practitioners through training and consultation to help them facilitate programs based around the use of board games and role playing games for deeper intentional purposes. We work with children, teens and adults, providing a variety of assistance for behavioral challenges as well as support for ADHD, Autism, anxiety and other needed fundamental goals. We have also published our own role playing game Branch Riders, designed for intentional goal work. We utilize the power of play to help people achieve their goals.

8. UDS Service Dogs

Services: Schools, Educational Programs, Advocacy, Therapeutic Services

Age Groups: Early Intervention (Ages 3-5), Elementary (Grades K-6), Secondary (Grades 7-12), Post Secondary (High School and Beyond)

Service dogs are dedicated companions that provide more than puppy love–they can help with deep pressure therapy, physical task such as: retrieval of items, alert a support person, and much, much more.

Our service dogs are trained from puppyhood to the age of at least two years old by a team of dedicated volunteers and professionals. Then, they’re partnered with individuals who have a mobility disability, autism, or Veterans with PTSD to help them live their possibility. Additionally, we train facility dogs to work in schools or other facilities, to help students or adults in a therapeutic manner, learn to navigate social and emotional obstacles of life.
